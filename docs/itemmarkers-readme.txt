These textures are colorized, modified weapon markers used by PigCell on his OA 0.8.8 stock map pxlfan. They are colored following this pattern:

- Weapons: Green
- Health: Pink
- Armor: Yellow
- Powerups: Cyan
- Holdables: Violet
- Runes: White (Neutral), Red (Red Team), Blue (Blue Team)

The assets are located in the folder itembases.

As for the source, the images are just recolors of one of PigCell's markers in the folder pxlfan. Nothing out of the ordinary was used, and the images themselves can be replaced.
